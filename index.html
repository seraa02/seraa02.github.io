About Me

I'm a Data & AI Engineer with an MS in Data Science from Texas A&M University, built on a Bachelor's in Artificial Intelligence & Data Science. Also an Aggie🤠

I work at the intersection of data engineering, applied AI, and product thinking. What drives me is taking a problem and turning it into something people can actually use — a pipeline that just runs, a dashboard that tells a story, a model that makes a real decision easier. I've built across healthcare, research, and analytics, and the same thread runs through all of it: closing the gap between what data can say and what people need to hear.

I think like an engineer and communicate like a product person. I tend to put myself in the other person's shoes — constantly asking whether something makes sense to whoever's on the receiving end, whether there's an edge case I'm missing, and whether I can make a tool feel less like software and more like it just works. That mindset pushes me to ship end-to-end: wrangling messy data, designing pipelines, and building interfaces non-technical users actually want to open. Privacy-constrained environments, real stakes, real users — that's where I do my best work.

And I love this era of building. With AI being able to go from idea to working product in days is exactly why I love this field.
