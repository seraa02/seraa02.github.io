About Me

I'm a Data & AI Engineer with an MS in Data Science from Texas A&M University, built on a Bachelor's in Artificial Intelligence & Data Science. Also an Aggie 🤠

I work at the intersection of data engineering, applied AI, and product thinking. What drives me is taking a problem and turning it into something people can actually use — a pipeline that just runs, a dashboard that tells a story, a model that makes a real decision easier.

I think like an engineer and communicate like a product person — constantly asking whether something makes sense to whoever's on the receiving end, whether there's an edge case I'm missing, and whether I can make a tool feel less like software and more like it just works.

Privacy-constrained environments, real stakes, real users — that's where I do my best work. And I love this era of building.
